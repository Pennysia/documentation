# Swap Tokens

::: info :information_source: INFO

- Currently available on private testnet only
- Make sure your wallet is connected before proceeding.
  :::

## Step 1 — Select Tokens

Choose the token you want to sell and the token you want to receive.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/SelectTokenSwap.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

## Step 2 — Enter Amount

Enter the amount you want to spend. The output amount is calculated automatically.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/swapStepTwo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

## Step 3 — Confirm & Execute

Review the quote, click **Swap**, approve in your wallet, and confirm the transaction.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/SwapStepThree.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

::: tip :book: TIP
If the swap fails due to price movement, open settings (⚙️) and increase your slippage tolerance, then try again.
:::
