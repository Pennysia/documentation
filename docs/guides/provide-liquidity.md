# Provide Liquidity

::: info :information_source: INFO
Make sure your wallet is connected before proceeding.
:::

## Step 1 — Select Tokens & Amounts

Choose the token pair and enter the amounts you want to deposit.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/selectTokenAmountLiquidity.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

## Step 2 — Choose Your Bias

Allocate your liquidity to a bucket — bullish on X, bullish on Y, or a 50/50 split.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/AddLiquidityStepTwo.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

## Step 3 — Confirm & Add

Review your position, click **Add Liquidity**, approve in your wallet, and confirm the transaction.

<video controls width="100%" style="border-radius: 12px; margin: 1rem 0;">
  <source src="/video/AddLiquidityStepThree.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>

::: tip :book: TIP
Not sure which direction to pick? Start with a 50/50 split to earn from both sides while you get familiar with the protocol.
:::
